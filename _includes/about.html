University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Urbana, IL, USA

I am a Ph.D. student in Geography at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and I am a member of Remote Sensing Space-Time Innovation Lab advised by Prof. Chunyuan Diao. My research focus on building geospatial solutions for rural development, food security and biodiversity and constructing environmental sensing models on real-world spatiotemporal data.

Prior to my PhD study, I received a Master degree in Environmental Policy from Duke University and Duke Kunshan University, advised by Prof. Binbin V. Li, working on spatial-explicit models and cartography for human-biodiversity nexus.
